Frequently Asked Questions

Ordering

How do I place an order?

You can place an order directly through our website. Browse our shop pages, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. For custom orders, please use the Custom Order Request Form or contact us directly.

What is the lead time for orders?

Standard orders typically require 2-3 days notice. Custom orders, especially for cakes, may require 1-2 weeks lead time depending on complexity. Please check product descriptions or contact us for specific timelines.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Modifications or cancellations may be possible if requested within 24 hours/1 day of placing the order and before production has begun. Please contact us as soon as possible with your order details.

Delivery & Pickup

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we offer delivery within Cabuyao and nearby areas (delivery fees apply and are calculated at checkout). We primarily use local third-party delivery services.

Can I pick up my order?

Yes, order pickup is available from our location in Cabuyao during business hours. Please select the pickup option during checkout and wait for confirmation that your order is ready before coming.

Products & Ingredients

Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options?

Currently, we have limited specific allergen-free options. Please check product descriptions carefully or contact us regarding specific ingredients or concerns. Note that all our products are made in a kitchen that handles common allergens like nuts, dairy, and gluten.

How should I store my baked goods?

Cookies are best stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5-7 days. Cakes and cupcakes should generally be refrigerated if not consumed within 1-2 days and brought towards room temperature before serving for best taste and texture. Specific instructions may vary per product.
