Our Story

Handcrafted treats made with love in Cabuyao since 2024

From Humble Beginnings

Ihurno Cookies started in 2024 in a small kitchen in Cabuyao, fueled by a lifelong passion for baking and sharing joy through delicious treats. What began as a hobby, baking for friends and family, quickly grew through word-of-mouth recommendations.

We believe that the best baked goods come from using quality ingredients, time-tested recipes, and a whole lot of love. Our commitment is to bring you cookies, cakes, and other delights that taste homemade, because they are.
